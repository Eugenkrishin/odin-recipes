Lasagna

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesnt have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce(you can buy it in groccery store or made it yourself)
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

easy steps to make a dish:

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.

Congrats! You cooked your first lasagna
